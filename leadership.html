Sri Patibandla

Founder and Chief Executive Officer

As an entrepreneur and founder, Srikiran (Sri) Patibandla brings over 25 years of technology management and execution experience in strategizing and delivering enterprise applications for customers. Srikiran, with his vision and execution ability, has transformed a boot-strapped organization to a most trusted advisor for Fortune 500 clients in delivering Cybersecurity solutions.

Under the distinguished leadership of Sri, TechDemocracy and its Cotelligent subsidiary have witnessed consistent growth of 40% over the last decade. Sri’s ability to foresee solutions for complex business problems led TechDemocracy to develop an innovative IT Risk Management software product called Intellicta, which provides cutting edge insights on threat intelligence and risk management for C-suite executives. Thanks to Sri’s visionary guidance, the company has been recognized by Gartner as a new entrant on their Magic Quadrant for IT Risk Management. The CyberWarriors Center of Excellence (CoE) is another exciting growth idea from Sri that is helping lead TechDemocracy into its next decade of growth.

Sri’s area of expertise includes enterprise IT applications and needs, corporate strategy, corporate management, technology services delivery, innovation, business operations and governance

Before the inception of TechDemocracy, Sri worked with Agilent technologies to streamline their infrastructure and IT operations to support enterprise applications. Prior to that Sri worked with Capgemini’s consulting group providing leadership and delivery of enterprise software for large enterprises. Sri earned a BS in Mechanical Engineering from Andhra University

Alex Lam

Chief Strategy and Business Development Officer

Alex is the Chief Strategy and Business Development Officer at TechDemocracy. Alex is responsible for driving business transformation for the company and heads up the Corporate Strategy, Partnerships/Alliances, and Sales/GTM for the company.

Alex is a technology business executive with over 25 years of experience across Enterprise IT Infrastructure, Services/Consulting, and Cybersecurity. Alex’s career has seen him take on progressing levels of responsibility across engineering, product management, strategy, and partnerships within major company organizations like Fujitsu, NetApp, Brocade, Quantum, and SanDisk. Prior to TechDemocracy, Alex was the Vice President and Head of the North America Strategy Office at Fujitsu where he led a revitalization of the company’s strategic business development in the US market.

Alex has been recognized as “Most Innovative People to Watch” by Technowize Magazine and has also received the ABA Silver Stevie Award for “IT Executive of the Year.” As part of his professional network, Alex has also served on an official Forbes Business Development Council member. Alex has an MBA from the Ross School of Business, University of Michigan, and a BS in Mechanical Engineering from the University of California, Berkeley.
