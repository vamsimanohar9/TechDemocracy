Schedule Free Consultation

CyberShield Identity Access & Governance Maturity (IAGM) Assessment

Analyze. Understand. Benchmark. Recommend.

Assess

Assess cybersecurity and risk maturity with Techdemocracy’s Cyber program assessment (CPA) methodology leveraging NIST Cyber Security Framework (CSF) 1.1 .

Understand

Data analyzed across observation and findings across all key subdomains in Identity Access and Governance parent categories.

Benchmark

Cybersecurity risk and maturity score developed for the customer and benchmarking scoring performed against industry peers (by vertical or similar company size).

Recommend

Security remediation roadmap created for the customer. Foundational capabilities will be identified with a 3-yr future state roadmap created for the customer, with key remediation activities identified.
