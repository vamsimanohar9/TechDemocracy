How we are helping customers with our SOC and Managed Services

SOC for State-level Government in India

TechDemocracy was brought in to implement the Cyber Security Operations Center for a major State in the country of India. As part of the solution, we handled all Threat monitoring and incident management & remediation across the state’s key IT infrastructure. Managed services were deployed to handle all device management and software maintenance.Additionally, we provided advisory services on Threat Surface Management and leveraged our Intellicta software platform to handle daily incident reporting and generation of metric dashboards for key stakeholders. As a true hybrid solution, our SOC was delivered across our combined consultant team that was integrated with the customer as well as solution delivery from our CyberWarriors CoE located in Hyderabad.
